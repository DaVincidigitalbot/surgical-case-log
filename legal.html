FERPA Compliance Notice

Educational Records Clarification

ClinicalCaseLog does not collect, store, or process student education records as defined by the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

What We Store

Our application stores only:

  • Clinical case documentation that you voluntarily create and enter
  • Your personal case tracking and learning logs
  • Self-reported procedure counts and clinical experiences

What We Do NOT Store

ClinicalCaseLog does not involve:

  • Academic grades or transcripts
  • Official institutional evaluation records
  • Faculty assessments or evaluations
  • Any records maintained by educational institutions

Institutional Data Sharing

We do not share your case data with educational institutions unless you explicitly authorize such sharing. The application is designed as a personal learning tool, and you control all aspects of your data sharing and privacy.

Student Rights

As this service does not maintain FERPA-covered education records, standard FERPA rights regarding inspection, amendment, and consent do not apply. However, you maintain full control over your clinical case data through our standard privacy controls outlined in our Privacy Policy.
