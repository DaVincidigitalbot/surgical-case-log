# Clinical Case Log, LLC — Formation Checklist

## Step 1: File Articles of Organization with NC Secretary of State
- [ ] Go to: https://www.sosnc.gov/online_services/Start/Business_Registration
- [ ] Entity type: Limited Liability Company (LLC)
- [ ] Name: Clinical Case Log, LLC
- [ ] Registered Agent: Graydon Stallard (your home address)
- [ ] Management: Member-managed
- [ ] Filing fee: $125 (credit card)
- [ ] Save confirmation/receipt

## Step 2: Get an EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- [ ] Go to: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- [ ] Apply as: Single-member LLC
- [ ] Free, instant — you get the number immediately
- [ ] Save/print the EIN confirmation letter (CP 575)

## Step 3: Open a Business Bank Account
- [ ] Bring: LLC filing confirmation + EIN letter + personal ID
- [ ] Recommended banks: Local credit union, Chase, or Bank of America
- [ ] Open a business checking account under "Clinical Case Log, LLC"
- [ ] Order a debit card for business expenses

## Step 4: Sign Operating Agreement
- [ ] Review: /products/caselog-app/legal/operating-agreement.md
- [ ] Fill in: Home address, initial cash contribution amount
- [ ] Print, sign, and date
- [ ] Keep on file (does NOT need to be filed with the state)

## Step 5: Update Payment Accounts
- [ ] Gumroad: Update business name and payout to LLC bank account
- [ ] Stripe (if used): Update to LLC business entity
- [ ] GitHub: Consider transferring repo to a business org

## Step 6: Annual Requirements
- [ ] NC Annual Report: Filed each year with Secretary of State ($200/year)
- [ ] Due by April 15 each year
- [ ] Federal taxes: Schedule C on personal return (disregarded entity)
- [ ] NC state taxes: Pass-through to personal return
- [ ] Consider S-Corp election (Form 2553) once profits exceed ~$40K/year

- [ ] Terms of Service / Privacy Policy for the app (I can draft these)
- [ ] Professional liability / cyber liability insurance (once contracts are signed)
- [ ] Consult with CPA for tax planning
